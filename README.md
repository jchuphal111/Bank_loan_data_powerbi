DASHBOARD 1 : Portfoli health (Summary)
**Business Purpose**: 
This page is designed for management to quickly evaluate whether loan growth is sustainable or risky. It combines growth metrics with credit quality indicators to prevent hidden default risk. 

It will help us to identify  : 
- We can identify is the bank growing safely?
- We can find out if the repayments keeping up with the lending or not.
- We can figure out the credit quality.
- It will help us to identify if we are approving too many risky borrowers.

==========================================================================================
Key indicators and their meanings :
- Loan growth vs recovery
  -Total Applications
  -Funded amount
  -Received amount
  IF the funded amount grows faster that the received amount then the future defaults risk increases.

- Good vs Bad Loans
  Good Loans : Fully Paind and Current
  Bad Loans : Charged Off
  This acts as the Bank's Health score

-Average DTI 
 It measures borrower repayment capacity
 Higher DTI = Higher default probability

-Average Interest Rate
 It indicates risk based pricing
 HIgher interest often means risky borrower segment

 -Monthly trend 
 Used to detect sudden policy mistakes seasonal risk spikes and collection performance drop 

- Loan status distribution table
 current ==> Healthy accounts
 Late ==> Early warning
 Charged off ==> Actual loss
 It indicates the operational monitoring of repayment lifecycle. This helps collection team act before default occurs.
==========================================================================================

Business decisions 
Using this page, management can 
-stop aggresive lending when bad loan rise
-Tighten approval rules for high DTI customers 
- Change interest rate strategy
- ALert collection team early and to investigate the risky regions

Insights
